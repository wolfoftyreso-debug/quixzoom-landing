How It Works

From mission to intelligence
in three steps

A seamless loop connecting the physical world with real-time optical analysis.

STEP 01

Missions Posted

Organizations post data capture missions directly on the map — specifying location, time window, requirements, and desired output.

STEP 02

Zoomers Capture

Zoomers nearby accept missions and complete them on their own schedule — with their phones. Fast, flexible, always-on coverage anywhere on earth.

STEP 03

Optical Analysis

Optic Insights processes every captured image through the optical analysis pipeline and delivers actionable intelligence directly to the client.
